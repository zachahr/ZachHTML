High School & Beyond

I graduated High School in 2019, and shortly afterward, I began taking classes at Arapahoe Community College Sturm Campus in Castle Rock! I chose this campus due to the unique collaboration between CSU and ACC, allowing me to obtain both my Associates Degree in Cybersecurity and pursue my Bachelor's Degree in Cybersecurity from CSU-Pueblo.

The ability to earn both degrees without accumulating excessive student debt was a goal I set for myself after high school. Student debt is a widespread issue in the United States, and I aimed to minimize its impact on my education. Last December, I earned my Associates Degree in Cybersecurity from ACC, and I am only one semester away from completing my Bachelor's Degree in Cybersecurity from CSU-Pueblo.
